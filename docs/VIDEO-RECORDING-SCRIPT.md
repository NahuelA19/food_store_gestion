## Recording Techniques for Smooth Demo

### Mouse Movement
- Move mouse slowly and smoothly
- Avoid jerky or rapid movements
- Use straight lines, not erratic paths

### Typing
- Type at a readable pace (not too fast, not too slow)
- Purposeful, confident clicks
- Natural pauses between actions

### Scrolling
- Scroll slowly (2-3 pixels at a time)
- Pause at key sections to let viewer read
- Avoid scrolling too far and having to scroll back

### Timing
- Wait 1-2 seconds for pages to load before moving on
- Don't rush - let viewers see what's happening
- Add 1-second pauses at key moments (buttons, totals, confirmations)

### Audio (Optional)
- If recording voiceover separately, keep it simple and professional
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Example voiceovers:
  - "Let's browse the product catalog"
  - "Adding item to cart"
  - "Proceeding to checkout"
  - "Order confirmed"

## If Re-recording Needed

### Common Issues:
- **Page didn't load**: Wait longer before next action
- **Typo in form**: Backspace and retype
- **Forgot a step**: Stop and restart from that section
- **Too fast/slow**: Adjust pacing on next attempt
- **Audio issues**: Re-record audio separately

### Solution:
- Do another full run-through
- It's OK to record multiple takes and use the best one
- Total recording time is short (3 minutes), so multiple attempts are quick
